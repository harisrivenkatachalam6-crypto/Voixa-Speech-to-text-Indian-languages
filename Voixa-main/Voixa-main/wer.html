How WER is Calculated

WER = (S + D + I) / N × 100%

S = Substitutions (wrong words)  ·  D = Deletions (missing words)  ·  I = Insertions (extra words)  ·  N = Total reference words.

WER compares your current session against the previous one. On your first recording, WER shows 0% as there is no prior session to compare against.
